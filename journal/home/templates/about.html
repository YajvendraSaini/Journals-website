About Us

Welcome to the International Journal of Ancient Sciences, Education, and Research (IJASER), a peer-reviewed, open-access platform dedicated to the exploration and dissemination of knowledge related to ancient sciences and their relevance in modern education and research.

At IJASER, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and contemporary scholarship by publishing high-quality research on ancient knowledge systems, including Vedic sciences, Ayurveda, astronomy, mathematics, philosophy, architecture, and more. Our journal fosters interdisciplinary studies and encourages dialogue between ancient practices and modern scientific advancements.

We are committed to advancing educational perspectives by showcasing scholarly articles that contribute to the understanding, teaching, and application of ancient sciences in the current era. IJASER serves as a global forum for educators, researchers, and practitioners to share insights, exchange ideas, and contribute to the growing body of knowledge in these fields.

Publication Guidelines

Authors Guidelines

The Journal of Ancient Sciences, Education, and Research (JASER) is a peer-reviewed, bi-annual publication by the International Association for Ancient Sciences. It publishes original research, theoretical, review, conceptual, and popular articles from global contributors.

Preparation of Manuscript

Manuscripts should be typed in English, double-spaced, and should not exceed 12 pages. Tables and figures must be numbered and placed on separate pages. All pages must be numbered, starting from the first page.

Submission

Submit your manuscript with a covering letter electronically to the email: editorjaser@gmail.com.

Publication Ethics

The Journal of Ancient Sciences, Education, and Research (JASER) is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of publication ethics. All stakeholders, including authors, reviewers, and editors, are expected to adhere to ethical principles.

Duties of Authors

  • Ensure originality and avoid plagiarism.
  • Provide accurate data and objective discussions.
  • Acknowledge sources and contributions.
  • Disclose conflicts of interest.

Duties of Reviewers

  • Maintain confidentiality.
  • Provide objective and constructive feedback.
  • Report ethical concerns.

Duties of Editors

  • Ensure fair and unbiased manuscript evaluations.
  • Maintain confidentiality of submissions.
  • Prevent conflicts of interest.
